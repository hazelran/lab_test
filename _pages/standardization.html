Standardization

VCP-Lab actively participates in international video coding standardization, contributing technical proposals and serving in leadership roles.

Standards Bodies

  • JVET (Joint Video Experts Team) — ITU-T VCEG & ISO/IEC MPEG joint committee developing VVC (H.266) and successor standards
  • MPEG (ISO/IEC JTC1/SC29/WG2) — contributing to FCVCM, EVC, and immersive video standards

Contributions & Activities

  • Prof. Choi served as Chairman of the Ad Hoc Group on Essential Video Coding (EVC) standard
  • Multiple input contributions to JVET meetings on AI-based coding tools and post-processing
  • Contributions to FCVCM (Feature Coding for Video Coding for Machines)
  • ISO/IEC Excellence Award (2022) — outstanding service to ISO/IEC JTC1/SC29
