Our Research

Our vision is to develop advanced video and media technologies aligned with international standards, leveraging AI and deep learning to push the frontiers of visual quality and compression efficiency.

Our research incorporates elements from deep learning, signal processing, computer vision, video coding standards, and international standardization bodies including MPEG and JVET.

Video Coding Standards

We actively contribute to international video coding standardization through JVET (Joint Video Experts Team) and MPEG (ISO/IEC JTC1/SC29). Our work spans VVC (H.266), HEVC (H.265), AVC (H.264), AV1, and EVC — developing faster algorithms, lower-complexity implementations, and novel coding tools. Prof. Choi served as Chairman of the Ad Hoc Group on the Essential Video Coding (EVC) standard and received the ISO/IEC Excellence Award (2022).

Team members: Prof. Kiho Choi, VCP-Lab members

AI-based Video Enhancement

We develop deep learning models — CNN, GAN, and Transformer architectures — to enhance the quality of compressed video and images as in-loop or post-processing filters. Our work focuses on multi-scale attention mechanisms, residual learning, and perceptual quality optimization for major codecs including VVC, HEVC, AV1, and EVC.

Team members: Tanni, Xilong, Junghyun, Zezhen Gai, Zhao Bopu, Prof. Choi

Video Coding for Machines (VCM)

As machine vision becomes pervasive in autonomous systems and smart infrastructure, we study how to compress visual data optimized for downstream AI tasks rather than human perception. This includes contributions to the MPEG FCVCM (Feature Coding for Video and Image Coding for Machines) standard, exploring efficient feature representations that preserve task-relevant information.

Team members: Xilong Liang, Prof. Choi

Immersive & Next-Generation Media

We look ahead to 4K/8K/16K ultra-high-resolution video, VR, AR, and 360° omnidirectional video — developing compression and processing technologies to support the next generation of immersive media. This includes our IITP-funded project on next-generation media compression for Metaverse content.

Team members: VCP-Lab members, Prof. Choi
