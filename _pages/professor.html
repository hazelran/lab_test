Bio

Prof. Kiho Choi leads the Video Coding and Processing Lab (VCP-Lab) at the Department of Electronics and Information Convergence Engineering, Kyung Hee University. His research focuses on video coding standards, AI-based video compression and enhancement, and next-generation immersive media technology.

He has actively contributed to international standards bodies including MPEG (ISO/IEC JTC1/SC29) and JVET (Joint Video Experts Team), serving as Chairman of the Ad Hoc Group on the Essential Video Coding (EVC) standard. In 2022, he received the Excellence Award from ISO and IEC in recognition of outstanding service to ISO/IEC JTC1/SC29.

VCP-Lab collaborates with industry partners including Samsung Electronics, LG Electronics, and Qualcomm, and is supported by IITP research grants.

Research Interests

  • Video coding standards (VVC, HEVC, AVC, AV1, EVC)
  • AI/DNN-based video and image compression and enhancement
  • Video Coding for Machines (VCM / FCVCM)
  • 4K/8K ultra-high-resolution and immersive video
  • International standardization (MPEG, JVET)
