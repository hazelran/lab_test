Welcome to the VCP-Lab!

Video Coding & Processing Lab is currently engaged in researching video, image, and media technologies that are aligned with international standards. With an eye toward social and technological changes, we are actively exploring advanced media options such as 4K/8K/16K videos and VR/AR related realistic videos, in order to anticipate the future of highly immersive media.

Our lab is committed to building greater competence in standardization, and we are eager to provide greater support toward the next media business. In doing so, we are opening doors to the future of media experiences.
